## The Core Idea

### The Simple Question

Before buying a memecoin, you want to answer ONE question:

> **"If I buy this token, what is the probability that I will lose my money due to a rug pull?"**

To answer this, we need to analyze the token's "health" based on measurable, on-chain data.
