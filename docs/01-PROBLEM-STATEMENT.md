## Executive Summary

**The Problem in One Sentence:**
> Traders have no reliable way to verify if a Solana memecoin is safe to buy before purchasing, resulting in 98.6% of tokens on platforms like Pump.fun being rug pulls or scams.

**The Scale of the Problem:**
- **$6 billion+** lost in Q1 2025 alone due to memecoin scams
- **98.6%** of Pump.fun tokens are rug pulls/pump-and-dumps
- **93%** of Raydium liquidity pools show "soft rug" characteristics
- **90%** of memecoin traders have lost money or made less than $100

## The Memecoin Rug-Pull Crisis

### What is a Memecoin?

A **memecoin** is a cryptocurrency that derives its value primarily from community interest, social media hype, and internet culture rather than underlying technology or utility. Examples include Dogecoin, Shiba Inu, and thousands of Solana-based tokens launched daily.

- **Low transaction fees** (~$0.00025 per transaction)
- **Fast confirmation times** (~400ms)
- **Easy token creation** (anyone can launch a token in minutes)
- **Pump.fun** platform making token creation accessible to everyone
